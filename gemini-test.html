Premium Jasmine Rice

Indulge in the naturally fragrant and long-grain Jasmine rice, perfect for a variety of culinary delights. Its delicate aroma and soft texture make every meal special.

Emata (Pearl Rice)

Discover the unique taste and texture of Emata rice, also known as Pearl rice. Its slightly sticky consistency is ideal for Asian cuisines.

High-Quality White Rice

Our selection of white rice offers a clean taste and versatile use. From everyday meals to special occasions, our white rice is a staple you can rely on.

Short Grain White Rice

Perfect for dishes requiring a slightly stickier texture, our short-grain white rice is carefully processed to ensure quality and taste.

Medium Grain White Rice

A balanced option, our medium-grain white rice cooks up tender and slightly chewy, making it suitable for a wide range of recipes.

Nutritious Parboiled Rice

Our parboiled rice retains more nutrients and offers a firmer texture. It's a healthy and delicious choice for your meals.
