About Shwe Myae Rice Trading

Founded in 2004, Shwe Myae Rice Trading is a family-owned business rooted in tradition and trust. Myanmar boasts a wide array of rice varieties, and from this abundance, we specialize in delivering the finest.

With a focus on freshness, quality, and customer satisfaction, we continue to build lasting relationships across Myanmar.

Decades of experience. One mission — to bring excellence to every table.
